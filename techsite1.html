About

Systems that can't be down.

I'm Justin Luis — a high-performance computing engineer and technical consultant. For the better part of a decade I've owned production HPC environments for aerospace & defense engineering programs: the clusters where structural analysis, CFD, and simulation workloads run around the clock and downtime has a dollar figure attached.

My work spans the full stack of a supercomputer — job schedulers and queue policy, high-speed interconnects, parallel filesystems, out-of-band hardware management, and the account-side reality of keeping a demanding customer's mission on schedule. I'm the person on site when a drive fails in a live array, when a fabric degrades, when a job that ran yesterday won't run today.

This site is my workbench in public: field notes from real incidents, tools I'm building, and a record of going deeper — currently into Cray EX, SLURM, and Slingshot territory.
